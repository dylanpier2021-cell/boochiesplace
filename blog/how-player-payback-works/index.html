How the Player PAYBACK Rewards Program Works

By the Boochie's team · February 26, 2026 · 4 min read

One of the nice perks of playing at Boochie's is Player PAYBACK, a rewards program that gives you points just for doing what you already enjoy. If you have ever wondered how it works, this quick guide covers everything from sign-up to redeeming your points.

What is Player PAYBACK?

Player PAYBACK is a free rewards program tied to the J&J Gaming app. When you check in and play at a participating location like Boochie's, you earn points. Those points add up over time and can be redeemed for merchandise. There is no membership fee and no catch, just a little something back for being a regular.

Step 1: Download the J&J Gaming app

Start by downloading the J&J Gaming app on your phone. Setting up your account takes only a few minutes. You will create your profile and be ready to start earning on your next visit.

Step 2: Check in at Boochie's

When you arrive at our Champaign location, open the app and check in. This is how the program knows you are here and ready to play. From that point, your visit starts working toward points.

The beauty of Player PAYBACK is its simplicity. Check in, play the games you already love, and watch your points grow in the app.

Step 3: Earn points as you play

As you enjoy slots and video poker, you accumulate points. The app keeps a running tally so you always know where you stand. There is nothing extra to do beyond playing and staying checked in.

Step 4: Redeem for merchandise

Once your points add up, you can redeem them for merchandise. The more you play over time, the more options you unlock. Ask our friendly staff about current choices, or browse them right in the app.

A few quick tips

  • Remember to check in every visit so you do not miss out on points.
  • Keep the app handy so you can glance at your balance any time.
  • Bring a valid ID, since you must be 21 or older to play and earn.
  • Ask staff if you have any questions. They are glad to help.

Play for fun, earn along the way

Player PAYBACK is a nice bonus, but the games themselves are entertainment first. Set a budget, play within it, and treat the points as a friendly extra rather than the goal. If you ever need support, help is available at 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537).
