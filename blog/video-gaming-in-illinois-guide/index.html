What Is Video Gaming in Illinois? A Beginner's Guide

By the Boochie's team · January 15, 2026 · 6 min read

If you have driven around Champaign or elsewhere in the state, you have probably seen signs for video gaming in Illinois. Maybe you have wondered what it actually is, whether it is the same as a casino, and what happens when you walk in. This guide answers those questions in plain language so you can decide if it is something you want to try.

Video gaming, explained simply

In Illinois, video gaming means playing on regulated machines called video gaming terminals, often shortened to VGTs. These are the slots-style reel games and video poker machines you find at licensed locations across the state. The state oversees these machines, which is why you will only see them at places that hold the proper license.

Think of it as a smaller, neighborhood-friendly version of the gaming floor you might picture at a big casino. Instead of a sprawling resort, you get a comfortable local spot where you can play a few games, enjoy a drink, and head home.

What kinds of games are there?

Most locations, Boochie's included, offer two main styles of play:

  • Slots-style reels. These are the spinning games with themes, symbols, and bonus rounds. They are easy to learn and great if you want to sit back and enjoy the action. You can read more on our slots and video gaming terminals page.
  • Video poker. These give you choices. You are dealt cards, decide which to keep, and your final hand is scored on a pay table. It is a favorite for players who like a little strategy. See our video poker page for details.

How old do you have to be?

You must be 21 or older to play video gaming in Illinois. This is a firm rule, so plan to bring a valid government ID, especially on your first visit. Staff may ask to see it, and that is simply part of keeping everything above board.

Video gaming is built for entertainment. The games are based on chance, no outcome is guaranteed, and the smart way to play is to set a budget and stick to it.

What to expect on your first visit

Walking into a place like Boochie's for the first time is easy. Here is the general flow:

  1. Come in during open hours. We are open every day from 6:00 AM to 2:00 AM.
  2. Have your ID ready in case staff ask to confirm you are 21 or older.
  3. Grab a free non-alcoholic drink. Coffee, soda, and water are on the house.
  4. Pick any open machine that looks fun and play at your own pace.
  5. Ask staff if you have questions. They are happy to walk you through anything.

Playing responsibly

Because the games are based on chance, the most important habit is to treat your play as entertainment and nothing more. Decide ahead of time what you are comfortable spending, take breaks, and never chase a result. If gambling ever stops feeling fun, help is available any time at 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537).

Ready to give it a try?

Video gaming in Illinois is meant to be approachable, and a friendly local spot is the best place to start. If you are in the Champaign-Urbana area, stop by Boochie's, say hello, and let us help you get comfortable. You can also join Player PAYBACK to earn points while you play.
