A Fun Night Out in Champaign: What to Know Before You Visit

By the Boochie's team · March 18, 2026 · 5 min read

Champaign has a fun, low-key energy, and a relaxed evening here does not have to be complicated. If you are thinking about stopping by Boochie's for some slots and video poker, here are a few friendly tips to make your visit smooth and enjoyable.

Pick your time

Boochie's is open every day from 6:00 AM to 2:00 AM, which gives you a lot of flexibility. Early mornings and weekday afternoons tend to be calmer if you like a quieter room. Evenings carry a bit more buzz. There is no wrong time, so choose whatever fits your mood and schedule.

Finding us and parking

We are located at 804 N. Randolph St., just north of downtown Champaign. The area is easy to reach from across the Champaign-Urbana region, whether you are coming from Urbana, Savoy, or farther out in Mahomet or Rantoul. Give yourself a minute to park and you will be inside in no time.

The best night out is a relaxed one. Come in, grab a free drink, and play at a pace that feels good to you.

What to bring

  • A valid ID. You must be 21 or older to play, so keep your ID handy.
  • Your phone. Download the J&J Gaming app ahead of time so you can check in for Player PAYBACK points.
  • A set budget. Decide what you are comfortable spending before you sit down.
  • A relaxed attitude. This is entertainment, so come ready to enjoy yourself.

Make the most of the little extras

Free coffee, soda, and water are part of the experience at Boochie's, so help yourself while you play. Our snack counter has chips and cold drinks if you want a quick bite. Prefer a calmer setting? Ask about our private gaming area for a more focused session.

Mix up your games

Part of the fun is variety. Spend some time on the slots, then switch to video poker when you want a change of pace. If you are still learning the ropes, our beginner's guide to Illinois video gaming is a quick read that covers the basics.

Keep it fun and safe

A great night out is one you feel good about the next day. Play within your budget, take breaks, and remember the games are for entertainment. If gambling ever stops being fun, confidential help is available any time at 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537).
