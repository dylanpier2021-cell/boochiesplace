A quick hop east on I-74

Mahomet sits just west of Champaign with a straight shot along I-74 connecting the two. For most players the drive is short and easy, an open stretch of highway that lands you near our part of town in no time. It is the kind of trip you can make on a whim when you feel like a little fun.

Boochie's gives Mahomet residents a comfortable, well-kept place to enjoy video gaming without driving far. Top machines, free coffee and soda, and the Player PAYBACK rewards program make the short drive more than worth it.
