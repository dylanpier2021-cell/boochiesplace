An easy run south to Champaign

Rantoul sits just north of Champaign, and the drive down is about as easy as it gets. US-45 connects the two directly, and I-57 offers a fast alternative when you want to make good time. Either way, you trade a short stretch of road for a relaxed seat in front of your favorite machine.

For Rantoul players who want more variety than a single stop closer to home, Boochie's is worth the trip. Our floor features current machines, the coffee is free, and Player PAYBACK rewards give you a reason to come back.
