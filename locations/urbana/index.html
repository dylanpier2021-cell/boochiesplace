An easy trip across town

Urbana and Champaign sit side by side, so getting to Boochie's is simple. Most folks coming from Urbana head west on University Ave. or Springfield Ave., then turn north toward Randolph St. In just a few minutes you go from your side of town to a comfortable seat in front of your favorite machine.

Plenty of our regulars call Urbana home. They like that Boochie's is close enough for a quick visit yet still feels like a real night out, with top machines, free drinks, and the Player PAYBACK rewards program adding a little extra to every trip.
