How video poker works

Video poker blends the feel of a slot machine with the logic of a card game. You are dealt five cards, you choose which to keep, and the rest are replaced. Your final hand is scored against a pay table shown right on the screen, so you always know what each hand is worth before you play.

Unlike pure reel games, video poker gives you choices that matter. Learning a few simple habits, like keeping a pair or chasing a strong draw, can make each session more engaging. It is a favorite for players who enjoy thinking through a hand.

  • Five-card draw style play you control
  • Clear on-screen pay tables for every game
  • Several game variations to choose from
  • Great for players who like a little strategy
