Player PAYBACK

Earn points just for playing

Player PAYBACK is the rewards program that gives you a little something back every time you visit. Check in through the J&J Gaming app, earn points as you play, and redeem them for merchandise. It is free to join and easy to use.

Sign Up at Boochie'sQuestions? Call 217-721-5533

How it works in three steps

1. Get the J&J Gaming app

Download the J&J Gaming app on your phone and set up your free Player PAYBACK account in just a few minutes.

2. Check in at Boochie's

When you arrive, check in through the app at our Champaign location. Your visits and play start earning points right away.

3. Redeem for merchandise

As your points add up, redeem them for merchandise. The more you play, the more you can choose from.

Why regulars love Player PAYBACK

Player PAYBACK turns the time you already spend playing into something extra. There is no cost to join, no complicated rules, and the app keeps your points organized so you always know where you stand.

Player PAYBACK questions

Does it cost anything to join?

No. Player PAYBACK is free to join. You only need the J&J Gaming app and a few minutes to set up your account.

How do I earn points?

Check in through the app when you visit Boochie's and earn points as you play. Your activity is tracked in the app so you can watch your points grow.

What can I redeem points for?

Points can be redeemed for merchandise. Ask our staff for the current options, or browse them in the app.

Do I need to be 21?

Yes. Illinois video gaming and the rewards tied to it are for adults 21 and older. Please bring a valid ID on your first visit.

Start earning on your next visit

Stop by Boochie's in Champaign, set up Player PAYBACK, and let every spin and hand work a little harder for you.
