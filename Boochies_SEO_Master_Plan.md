## 4. Google Business Profile — 10 GMB posts/month (the biggest local lever)

For a gaming café, **the map pack is where the customers are.** GBP optimization beats almost everything else.

**Profile checklist (one-time):**
- Primary category: **Casino** (add secondary: "Video arcade" / "Gaming café" if offered).
- Complete: hours (incl. "closes 2 AM"), phone, website, attributes (21+, free Wi-Fi, restroom, free snacks/drinks if applicable, accessible).
- Photos: upload the 4 Cloudinary images + add new ones monthly (Google favors fresh photos). Geotag where possible.
- Services/Products: list "Slot machines," "Video poker," "Video gaming terminals," "Complimentary snacks & drinks."
- Q&A: seed 5–6 common questions (Are you 21+? Do you serve drinks? What hours? Is there free play?) and answer them.
- Add the business description with primary keywords (≤750 char).

**10 posts/month formula** (rotate these types — ~2–3 per week):
1. **Offer post** ×2 — e.g., "Free $10 slot play after 10 visits" / weekday promo.
2. **What's New** ×3 — new games added, jackpot winners (with permission), remodel, fresh snacks.
3. **Event/Update** ×2 — weekend hours, holiday hours, "Friday night = busiest, come early."
4. **Photo/Highlight** ×2 — a featured machine, the lounge, a winner photo.
5. **FAQ/Educational** ×1 — "How video gaming terminals work" (links to a blog post).

Every GMB post: 1 photo, 1 primary keyword used naturally (e.g., "slots in Champaign"), a CTA button (Call / Learn more), and a link to the matching website page. Keep each ~150–300 characters.

## 6. Backlinks — $50/month (quality over quantity)

At $50/mo, aim for **1–3 genuinely good links**, not 50 spam links (those get the site penalized). Targets that fit a local gaming café:

- **Local news / community:** The News-Gazette, Smile Politely (Champaign-Urbana culture mag), local "things to do" roundups.
- **Champaign County Chamber** member link (also a citation).
- **Local sponsorships:** sponsor a local team/event/charity → most list sponsors with a link. Cheap, white-hat, very local.
- **Partnerships:** nearby bars, restaurants, barbershops — cross-link "things to do nearby."
- **Niche relevance:** Boochie's video-gaming terminal operator's location directory; legitimate Illinois gaming café roundup blogs.
- **HARO / Connectively / Featured:** answer reporter queries about Illinois video gaming / small business.
- **Guest posts** (see §7) double as backlinks.

Avoid: PBNs, fiverr "10,000 backlinks," casino link farms, anything offshore-gambling related. For a real local business, those do more harm than good.

## 8. Compliance & trust (non-negotiable for this niche)

Bake these into the site and every post — they also build E-E-A-T (trust) which Google rewards:
- **"Must be 21+ to play"** visible on home, footer, and GBP.
- **Responsible gaming line** in the footer of every page and the foot of every blog/GMB post:
  > *Must be 21+. Please play responsibly. If you or someone you know has a gambling problem, call 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537) or text "ILGamb" to 833234.*
- **Display the video gaming license number** in the footer (competitors like Libby's show "License No. VL-00xx-xx"). Get Boochie's license # and add it.
- Don't make guaranteed-winnings claims. Frame as entertainment.
