About us

A friendly place to play in Champaign

Boochie's Slots and Video Poker, also known as Boochie's Place, is a licensed Illinois video gaming spot built around one idea: a little entertainment should feel easy, welcoming, and fun.

Our story

Boochie's opened its doors on N. Randolph St. to give the Champaign area a comfortable, well-kept place to enjoy video gaming close to home. We are not a giant casino floor and we like it that way. We are the neighborhood spot where the staff remembers your name and the coffee is always on.

Every detail, from the lighting to the seating to the snack counter, was chosen to make your visit relaxed. Whether you have ten minutes or a couple of hours, we want it to feel good to walk through the door.

What you can expect

A clean, welcoming room

We keep the place bright, tidy, and comfortable so you can settle in and enjoy yourself.

Free non-alcoholic drinks

Coffee, soda, and water are on the house while you play. Help yourself and stay a while.

Snack counter

Grab chips and a cold drink from the counter when you want a quick bite between rounds.

Private gaming area

Prefer a quieter session? Our private gaming area gives you space to focus and relax.

Top-tier machines

We feature current terminals from leading manufacturers, with fresh titles added regularly.

Player PAYBACK rewards

Earn points as you play and redeem them for merchandise through the J&J Gaming app.

Play smart, play 21+

Illinois video gaming is for adults 21 and older, and we take that seriously. Boochie's is a place for entertainment, not a promise of winnings. We encourage every guest to set a budget, take breaks, and keep the fun in perspective.

Need support? Gambling problem? Call 1-800-GAMBLER (1-800-426-2537). Confidential help is available 24/7.
