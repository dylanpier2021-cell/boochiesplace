What are Illinois video gaming terminals?

In Illinois, the slots-style machines you play at a licensed location are called video gaming terminals, or VGTs. They work much like the reel games you picture at a casino: choose your game, set your play level, and spin. The state regulates these machines, so you can relax and focus on the fun.

At Boochie's, our terminals are current models kept in great shape. We rotate in fresh titles so regulars always have something new to try, from classic three-reel looks to modern themed games with bonus features.

  • Easy to learn, no experience needed
  • Choose your own pace and play level
  • Regulated, licensed machines you can trust
  • New game themes added regularly
